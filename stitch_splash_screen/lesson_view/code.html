Introduction to Algebra

This lesson introduces the fundamental concepts of algebraic expressions. We will explore variables, coefficients, and the basic operations used to manipulate these expressions.

Quiz: Check Your Understanding

In the expression 5x + 3, what is the coefficient?

cancel

Incorrect

The coefficient is the number multiplied by the variable. In this case, '5' is multiplied by 'x'.

Lesson Summary

  • Variable: A symbol (like x or y) that represents a value.
  • Coefficient: A number used to multiply a variable.
  • Expression: A combination of variables, numbers, and operators.
